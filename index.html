ABOUT GAME

A complete gaming metaverse of NFT Dragons and Knights

Legends of Aragon is a game, where players will embody the role of the Templar Knight who will prove his skill and worth by raising dragons and mythical beasts. His beasts will engage in hand-to-hand combat to win trophies in the form of nft tokens.

WATCH THE REVIEW

How it Works

The idea of the game is to collect your beasts team of 5 dragons and send them to fight. The mystery box allows you to get your dragon by chance and if you have more lucky points your chance for a better dragon is higher.

There are 3 types of mystery boxes:

BRONZE - dragons can be bought in BEPR tokens

SILVER - dragons can be bought in LOAG tokens

GOLDEN - dragons are available with lucky points only

Lucky points can be earned by doing dayly activities - play the game, send dragons to hunt, use NFT marketplace and tournaments. As the result - best dragon team can earn tournament and get reward in BUSD

PvP mode

This mode allows to upgrade the dragons, create interactions through the exchange of in-game assets; providing them with a real-life economy

Grow dragons

You can improve your dragons by sending them to merging center. It is required to have 5 same type (name and colour) dragons that will be changed to a new better one with improved skills. Doing that several times you can significantly increase a fighting, health and staking power of your beasts

Hunting

Hunting allows player to earn money in a passive way. When you send a dragon to hunt, it start bringing your income within 300-900%APR. Minimum staking period is 3 days

Play and Earn

You can trade your beasts for cryptocurrencies. By trading, you can increase the value of their in-game assets. With a help of marketplace you can find a dragon your need for merging centre or to sell the dragons you do not need

Aragon’s Beasts

Different types of dragons have different power including attack, defense, health and staking rate. 1 star bronze dragon is weaker than 1 star Silver dragon. The Golden Dragon is the most powerful except a dragon boss.

Capable of spitting fire and often flying; dragons have a magical connection to the forces of nature and the universe. Templar knights must prove their worth by raising dragon eggs that will grow into ferocious beasts, capable of defeating their peers in tournaments.

Each victory will improve the chances of receiving greater prizes. Dragons can reach seven levels, with each level increasing the reward potential.

Dragons

These gigantic and fabulous reptiles have been hated and adored with equal ardor in all cultures. Capable of spitting fire and often flying; dragons have a magical connection to the forces of nature and the universe.

Popular Kinds

1

Kaida "Little Dragon"

Feature:

FAST REGENERATION

Power:

spits Greek Fire,
that does not die out

Although small, the scales that cover its body are of unparalleled strength. This reptilian-looking one with bat-like wings spits Greek Fire; a flame that does not die out. Its damage is permanent, and every second exposed to its attack brings you closer to eternal death. Because of its ability to regenerate in seconds, defeating Kayda is almost impossible.

2

Wyvern (Tessylth "The Ice Lady")

Feature:

Light as the wind

Power:

expels fire and a blast of cold
wind that freezes her enemies

Many have been the stories about the exploits of the great dragons, such as Fafnir, or Smaug, but very few describe the ferocity of the female dragons. Tessylth, of the Wyvern race, expels fire and a blast of cold wind that freezes her enemies. Light as the wind, she can protect herself from enemy fire by flapping her wings and creating a curtain of air.

3

Fey Dragon (Glaurung "The Deceiver")

Feature:

Immune to magic

Power:

spits green fire and the tips of their
claws possess a deadly poison

Don't let its flying lizard look fool you. Fey Dragons spit green fire and the tips of their claws possess a deadly poison. Immune to magic, they can be truly fearsome if properly trained. Glaurung's only weakness is in close combat.

4

Asian (Zu "The Stormbird")

Feature:

High intelligence

Power:

spits water, their attack slows
down enemies

Although mythology describes them as benevolent dragons, they are neutral beings that can cause great destruction when enraged. Their remarkable chin allows them to balance and orient themselves in the air and against wind currents. Unlike western dragons, this oriental race spits water, so their attack slows down enemies and stuns them. Its power does not lie in strength but in intelligence; Zu is a fast and intuitive dragon when hunting.

5

Hydra (Bryzud "The Lord of Fire")

Feature:

Renowned for their cannibalism

Power:

Spews flames from
different sides

The hydra (Draco hydra) is the only race among dragons, which reproduces by separating heads from its body. They fight using flames that spit from multiple directions, and their bites can be deadly. Renowned for their cannibalism, Bryzud is feared, even by other dragon races.
