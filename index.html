ABOUT GAME

A complete gaming metaverse of NFT Dragons and Knights

Legends of Aragon is a game, where players will embody the role of the Templar Knight who will prove his skill and worth by raising dragons and mythical beasts. His beasts will engage in hand-to-hand combat to win trophies in the form of nft tokens.

How it Works

The idea of the game is to collect your beasts team of 5 dragons and send them to fight. The mystery box allows you to get your dragon by chance and if you have more lucky points your chance for a better dragon is higher.

There are 3 types of mystery boxes:

BRONZE - dragons can be bought in BEPR tokens

SILVER - dragons can be bought in LOAG tokens

GOLDEN - dragons are available with lucky points only

Lucky points can be earned by doing dayly activities - play the game, send dragons to hunt, use NFT marketplace and tournaments. As the result - best dragon team can earn tournament and get reward in BUSD

PvP mode

This mode allows to upgrade the dragons, create interactions through the exchange of in-game assets; providing them with a real-life economy

Grow dragons

You can improve your dragons by sending them  to merging center. It is required to have 5 same type (name and colour) dragons that will be changed  to a new better one with improved skills. Doing that several times you can significantly increase a fighting, health and staking power of your beasts

Hunting

Hunting allows player to earn money in a passive way. When you send a dragon to hunt, it start bringing your income within 300-900%APR. Minimum staking period is 3 days

Play and Earn

You can trade your beasts for cryptocurrencies. By trading, you can increase the value of their  in-game assets. With a help of marketplace you can find a dragon your need for merging centre or to sell the dragons you do not need
